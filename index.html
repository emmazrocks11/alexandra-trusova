Alexandra Trusova

"The Russian Rocket"

  • Full Name: Alexandra Vyacheslavovna Ignatova née Trusova
  • Date of Birth: June 23, 2004 (age 20)
  • Birthplace: Ryazan, Ryazan Oblast, Russia
  • Hometown: Moscow, Russia
  • Country: Russia
  • Skating Club: FS Academy Angels of Plushenko
  • Coach: Evgeny Plushenko

Biography

Trusova is renowned for her exceptional quadruple-jumps, and made history as the first female skater to successfully land a quad lutz, flip, and toe loop in competition. She is also the first to achieve two — later three — quads in a free skate program. She is the Beijing 2022 Olympic silver medallist, a bronze medallist at the 2021 World Figure Skating Championships, and a bronze medallist at the 2020 European Championships.
